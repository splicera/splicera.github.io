Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SPLICERA's training and consulting services

What is Microsoft Copilot Studio?

Microsoft Copilot Studio is a low-code platform for building conversational AI agents and automating workflows. It enables businesses to create intelligent chatbots, automate repetitive tasks, and integrate AI capabilities across Microsoft 365, Teams, and other enterprise systems without extensive programming knowledge.

Who should attend SPLICERA workshops?

Our workshops are designed for business analysts, IT professionals, operations managers, digital transformation leads, and anyone responsible for process improvement or automation initiatives. No prior AI or coding experience is required for Tier 1 workshops, though basic familiarity with business processes is helpful.

Are workshops delivered remotely or in-person?

All workshops are delivered remotely by default, serving clients worldwide. In-person delivery is available upon request and may incur additional travel and accommodation costs. Remote sessions are conducted via your preferred virtual meeting platform (Teams, Zoom, etc.).

What technology do we need to participate?

Participants need a computer with internet access, a virtual meeting platform (Microsoft Teams, Zoom, or similar), and ideally access to Microsoft Power Platform or Copilot Studio for hands-on workshops. We'll provide detailed technical requirements upon booking.

How quickly can we see ROI from automation?

Many clients see initial time savings within weeks of implementing their first Copilot Studio agent. Comprehensive automation initiatives typically show measurable ROI within 3-6 months, depending on scope and complexity. Our workshops include ROI calculation frameworks to help you track and measure success.

What happens after the workshop?

You'll receive comprehensive documentation including workshop summaries, implementation roadmaps, and recommended next steps. Follow-up support options are available, and we can discuss ongoing consulting or additional training as your automation journey progresses.

Can workshops be customized for our industry?

Absolutely. We tailor all workshops to your specific industry context, whether you're in finance, healthcare, retail, manufacturing, or professional services. During pre-workshop planning calls, we gather information about your unique challenges and customize examples and use cases accordingly.

What's your cancellation policy?

Workshops must be booked at least 7 days in advance. Cancellations made more than 72 hours before the scheduled workshop receive a full refund. Cancellations within 72 hours may be subject to a 50% fee. We're happy to reschedule at no additional charge when possible.
