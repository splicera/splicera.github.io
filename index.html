What Happens in the Workshop

Lab 1: Brainstorming & Idea Development

Students practice using ChatGPT or Microsoft Copilot for early-stage thinking—exploring topics, refining questions, and structuring potential essays without letting AI do the thinking for them.

Lab 2: Refining Your Argument

Learn to use ChatGPT or Microsoft Copilot as a critical thinking partner to test logic, identify weaknesses, and strengthen reasoning while maintaining intellectual ownership.

Lab 3: Feedback & Revision

Students discover how to get actionable feedback on clarity, flow, and structure without losing their personal voice or accepting AI rewrites automatically.

Lab 4: Study & Productivity Support

Explore ChatGPT for active learning—summarizing readings, creating self-test questions, and designing study plans that reinforce understanding.

Lab 5: Building Your Prompt Library

Students create a personalized collection of ethical prompts they can use throughout their academic journey, categorized by task and learning stage.

All labs include reflection questions, group discussion, and real-time practice with immediate facilitator guidance.
